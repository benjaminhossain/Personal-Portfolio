About Me

Hi my name is Benjamin Hossain. I am a recent graduate from Stony Brook University with a Psychology degree. I have 3+ years of experience with product development working with a start-up company based in New York. I am highly experienced with SDLC, Agile methodology, Sprint, Scrum, Risk Management, and Budget.

I am also an aspiring Front End Web Developer, experienced with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.JS, Express, Webpack, Sass and more. I am also a painter and therefore I find Front End Development extremely amusing. I hope to learn more about Web Development and eventually become a fullstack developer.

I love traveling the world. I have done a few solo trips to South America and really enjoyed. I reckon traveling to new and unknown places has made a better individual who is very adaptable to change and have the ability to work in a new environment. I love learning new languages and cultures, which has made me a better learner and open-minded to others. I wish to travel around the globe someday.
